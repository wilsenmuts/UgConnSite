BRIEFING

This module enables you to pay bills or make other payments remotely in case one lacks a means in which to do so. UgCon provides you with an agent to manage and follow up your payment plan. Transparency is key; meaning you get to follow up on every step. It should be noted that such activities take time as UgCon has to consider the agent to use or get someone else in a process of 1 to 2 weeks from initialization.
