Build With Us Your Ugandan Dream
Are you looking for a trustworthy partner to help you do anything within Uganda?

Many people who send resources like money to someone in Uganda to help them do activities like building a house, starting a business and so on; report cases of misuse or even theft of that money by their 'trusted compatriots'. As a result many people who have dreams decide to first move into Uganda to do these activities only to run out of resources with time and yet they let go of the source of capital when they moved in. This is where Reconna comes in to help any individual do anything within Uganda without necessarily being around. Reconna is a partner you can trust with its self-driven and client intensive approach to help you achieve your Uganda dream remotely.





About Us


We are a team built to help you start any commercial activity within Uganda

Reconna is a Uganda-based company that is aimed at enabling different individuals outside Uganda to start businesses, buy properties, sell properties or even do any other activities without necessarily being on site within Uganda. We are an organized team of agents from different sectors ranging from business, technology, law and so on. Our work is to receive your activity request, process its requirements as soon as possible, get back to you and start off the activity with transparency, honesty and active involvement of both parties. Reconna strives to ensure that its clients get what they need, the way they need it and at the most appropriate time of delivery.

Why Us






Transparency And Trust

Reconna is built upon a business model that has been revised in such a manner that cares for the trust in the process of fulfilling its activities. Trust means that the money provided is used in a transparent and satisfactory way. The client also is provided with tools to follow up his or her projects while they are being catered for by our agents


Expertise And Experience

Reconna has its team of agent ready to receive your request. Our Agents are assigned activities to handle basing on their expertise and experience in different fields. With this advantage we can guarantee you that the work will not be shoddy or carelessly done. Aside from that Reconna is actively signing partnerships with company which have experienced people with expert knowledge in different fields.


Costs and Money Management

Money is the heart of almost every activity nowadays. Reconna will handle all the money you provide in carefully and with transparency. We also provide you with an account to help you save up your money for any activity when you have started at least one activity with us.
